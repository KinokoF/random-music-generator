About

Hey there! I'm Francesco Bigozzi, and I built Random Music Generator to bring a bit of musical randomness into your life. This project is open-source, which means you can dive into the code, explore how it works, and even contribute if you're up for it! You can check out the project’s repository here and help make it even better.

What’s under the hood?

  • Angular for the framework.
  • Angular Material for those slick UI components.
  • Dexie.js to handle local storage of your music creations.

Shout-out to the libraries that make it all possible:

  • random for generating random numbers.
  • tonal for scales, chords, and all things music theory.
  • spessasynth_lib for playing the MIDI files.
  • JZZ for creating MIDI files.
  • FluidR3 for those sweet sound fonts.

And a quick nod to Three.js, Faker, and ng-web-apis for their small but helpful contributions.

Feel free to reach out to me at kinokoeffe@outlook.com if you have any questions or ideas. Let's make something cool together!
